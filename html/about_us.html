About Us

TAR Cinemas was co-founded by Lim Fang Chern, Lim Xin Jie, Tang Yan Chun, Tham Qi Lun and Winnie Chiong Yan.

Before that, for the reason why we started it are when we watching television, laptop, ipad etc, we can't get the feeling of being immersed with the movies. However, cinema exactly satisfies this part of the demand.According to the data representation, cenima still is the first choice among the youth in this modern age. About it, 57.97% of the respondents choose movie cinema among their favorite offline leisure and entertainment activities. Even under the influence of the epidemic, more than 90% of the respondents have been to the theater at least once in the past year. The above data gives us an idea to set up this cinema.

In order to enhance the viewing experience of customers, we have included somethings:

  1. BIG Screen, complete with large format display and provided a good visual for a 3D movie ;
  2. Movie Options, choice of multiple movie genres so can optionally choose the movies which like to watch;
  3. Massive Speaker System, the quality of sound is important requirement that why customers will return visit;
  4. Food & Beverage Combo, easily to satisfy yours imagine and shared with friends or enjoy the times yourself happily.
