 Mad Fate 命案 （粤）
  Release Date : 19 October 2023   Spoken Language : CAN

  Genre : Thriller / Mystery   Classification : 16

Director

Cast

Synopsis

A fortune teller with a complicated past meets a young man that emanates a murderous aura. He tries to intervene before the man can strike, but he gets to the scene of the crime too late. Undeterred, he continues to challenge fate as he tries to save this young man from going down a dark path. However, the young man`s bloodthirsty desires force the fortune teller to put his sanity on the line in this suicide mission.
